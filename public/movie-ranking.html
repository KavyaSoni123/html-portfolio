The Best Movies According to Kavya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

12th Fail

12th Fail is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language biographical drama film produced, written and directed by Vidhu Vinod Chopra. It is based on the 2019 eponymous non-fiction book by Anurag Pathak about the real-life story of Manoj Kumar Sharma, who overcame extreme poverty to become an Indian Police Service officer.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 movie about a team of astronauts traveling to another galaxy to find a new home for humanity. The movie explores themes of love, sacrifice, the resilience of the human spirit, and the relationship between time and space.

Evil Dead Rise

Evil Dead Rise is a 2023 American horror film that tells the story of two estranged sisters who must fight for survival against flesh-possessing demons. The film is the fifth installment in the Evil Dead series and stars Lily Sullivan and Alyssa Sutherland.
